Games

These are my toy game projects - simple experiments where I tinker around with game mechanics and interactive animations. Nothing fancy, just playful coding adventures!

Snake

Classic Snake game where you control a snake to eat food and grow longer. Avoid hitting the walls or yourself! Use arrow keys to navigate and try to achieve the highest score.

Deployed: March 2024 Play

Falling Sands

A creative sandbox game where you can manipulate falling sand and other particles. Use your mouse or touch controls to interact with the environment.

Deployed: March 2024 Play
